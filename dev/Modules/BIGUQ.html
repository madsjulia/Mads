BIGUQ.jl

BIGUQ.jl implements Bayesian Information Gap Decision Theory (BIGDT) for Uncertainty Quantification, Experimental Design and Decision Analysis.

Relevant examples:

  • Information Gap Analysis
  • Decision Analysis
  • Model Diagnostics

BIGUQ.jl functions:

BIGUQ.getmcmcchain — Method

Get MCMC chain

BIGUQ.makebigdts — Method

Make BIGDT analyses for each possible decision assuming that the proposed observations proposedobs are observed

BIGUQ.makebigdts — Method

Makes BIGDT analyses for each possible decision assuming that no more observations will be made
